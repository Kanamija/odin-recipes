Miso Soup Recipe

Description

Miso soup is a traditional Japanese soup consisting of a savory broth called “dashi,” mixed with softened miso paste, and often containing ingredients such as tofu, seaweed, and green onions. It is enjoyed both as a daily comfort food and as part of traditional meals in Japan.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dashi (Japanese soup stock)
  • 3 tablespoons miso paste
  • 1/2 cup cubed tofu
  • 1 sheet nori (seaweed), cut into strips
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onions

Steps

  1. Prepare the dashi by heating it in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add the miso paste to a small bowl, then whisk in a little hot dashi until smooth.
  3. Stir the dissolved miso mixture back into the pot of dashi.
  4. Add tofu cubes and nori strips, and simmer gently for a few minutes.
  5. Remove from heat, garnish with chopped green onions, and serve hot.
  6. Pair with fish and a fresh bowl of Japanese rice.
